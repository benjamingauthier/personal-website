LastTram

Technology: iOS, Android
LastTram is a mobile application which provides the hour of the last tramway for your nearest stop. Moreover, you can schedule an alert in order not to miss your tramway. Currently available in six French cities : Angers, Bordeaux, Brest, Nantes and Nice, we will launch the application in more than 250 cities in the world. LastTram has been downloaded more than 4K times and is used more than 80 times each day.
